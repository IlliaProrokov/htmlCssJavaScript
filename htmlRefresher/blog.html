Conclusion: Better Accessibility and SEO

By properly implementing semantic structure, we improve the overall quality of the webpage. This directly benefits users with disabilities, as screen readers can better navigate and announce content hierarchy. Furthermore, search engines can more accurately index the page, improving its ranking.

It is a foundational best practice that every web developer should embrace from the start of their journey. Always choose a semantic tag over a generic one when its meaning aligns with your content's purpose.
